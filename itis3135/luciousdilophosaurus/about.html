Who are we?

In 1492 when I sailed the ocean blue, I came across an octopus in my fishing net and unfortunately it passed away before I could throw it back. I used this as an opportunity to take its teeth. When my first mate offered a bountiful amount of dabloons for the tooth I knew I had the idea of a century. I have been selling teeth ever since.
