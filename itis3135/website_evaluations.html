Website Evaluations

svz.io

SVZ.io has been nominated for having the best webflow. With nice colors and well spaced boxes that are put together nice and neat it makes for a website that has a nice look to it.

  • Contrast: The contrast works extremely well with the dark background and lighter colors throughout.
  • Reptition: The colors, fonts, and size of each element on this page remain consistent.
  • Alignment: This webpage does not go for traditional alignment, instead opting for a repeating pattern making the alignment flow well.
  • Proximity: All the information is near alike information. As you scroll down you go through distinct sections of information.

arngren.net

Arngren.net is chaotic mess of different colors and boxes scattered about trying to sell items without caring about how it looks.

  • Contrast: The contrast is a mess, there are light and dark colors mixed throughout that do not have order.
  • Reptition: While fonts do seem to stay consitent, the size and color of different elements change throughout.
  • Alignment: There is no alignment pattern, everything is just thrown in to a big canvas and hope it fits.
  • Proximity: Everything is everywhere you need to know where to look to find what you need.
